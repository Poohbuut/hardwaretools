FAQ

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to over 40 countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination and are shown at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day no-questions return policy on all items. Tools must be unused and in original packaging.

Do you offer bulk pricing?

Yes, for orders of 10 or more units. Contact us with your requirements and we will prepare a custom quote.

Are your tools covered by warranty?

All hand tools carry a lifetime guarantee against manufacturing defects. Power tool accessories carry a 2-year warranty.
